Surfaces & Access

Hollow Zero is a cross-surface assistant focused first on important email, scheduling, follow-ups, and other high-signal tasks. The goal is not another chat tab. It is a system that notices what matters and routes the next step to your app, watch, Telegram, or Mac.

No. The watch still matters, but it is now companion hardware inside the broader Hollow Zero system. The main product is continuity across surfaces and outcomes that keep moving, not a watch-only experience.

Privacy & Connectors

The direction is user-owned account connections whenever the upstream API supports them. That means the user connects their own account instead of relying on a developer-funded shared API key.

Not by default. Public connectors should move toward user-owned accounts, bring-your-own credentials, or clear creator-hosted pricing instead of quietly charging developers for every end-user action.
