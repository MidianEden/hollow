Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Hollow 0W.

Compatibility & Setup

Currently, the Hollow companion app is Android-only. An iOS version is planned for the future, but for this prototype run, you will need an Android device to set up and configure the watch.

Privacy & Security

Privacy is our core philosophy. We do not sell your voice data or usage history. Voice queries are encrypted in transit. We minimize data retention to only what is necessary to service your request.

Hardware & Features

Pricing & Subscriptions
