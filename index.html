Core Components

  • MCU: Espressif ESP32-S3 (Dual-core Xtensa LX7, 240MHz)
  • Memory: 8MB PSRAM, 16MB Flash
  • Display: 1.54" Color TFT LCD (240240), Capacitive Touch
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 5.0 BLE
  • LoRa Module: SX1262 (868MHz/433MHz/915MHz)

Sensors & Peripherals

  • Accelerometer: BMA423 3-axis
  • Audio: PDM Microphone, MAX98357A Amplifier
  • Haptic: DRV2605 Haptic Motor
  • RTC: Real-Time Clock
  • IR: Infrared Transmitter (S3 Plus variant)

Power & Battery

  • Battery: ~400-500mAh LiPo
  • Charging: A-USB Charging
  • Water Resistance: IP54 (splash-proof)

Platform

  • OS: Custom HollowOS (Closed Source)
  • Updates: Over-the-air firmware updates
  • Focus: Speed and Simplicity
